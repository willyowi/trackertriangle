TRIANGLE-TRACKER


What to know!

  • Triangles are polygons with the least possible number of sides (three).
  • The three internal angles of a triangle always add to 180 degrees.
  • An equilateral triangle has three sides of equal length and three equal angles.
  • An isosceles trianglehas two sides of equal length and two equal angles.
  • A scalene trianglehas 0 sides of equal length and no equal angles.
  • Any polygon, such as a square, octagon or pentagon, can be divided into a series of triangles.
  • The longest side of the triangle is called the hypotenuse.
